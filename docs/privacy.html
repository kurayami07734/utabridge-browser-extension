Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

UtaBridge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by UtaBridge when you use our browser extension.

UtaBridge is a browser extension that provides real-time translation and romanization of Japanese song titles on Spotify. By using UtaBridge, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

App Name: UtaBridge

When you use UtaBridge, we collect the following information:

  • Authentication Data (via Google OAuth): Email address, name, and profile picture. This data is stored for account identification and authentication purposes only.
  • Song Metadata (for translation): Song title, album name, and artist name are sent to our server to provide translation and romanization services via Google Cloud Translation API. This metadata may be cached in our database to optimize API calls (avoiding repeated translations for the same songs).

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process your information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and identify users for extension functionality
  • To provide real-time translation and romanization of Japanese song titles
  • To improve and maintain the extension's performance and features

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our extension (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Data Retention

Authentication Data: Stored indefinitely for account management until you request deletion.

Song Metadata Cache: Song title, album name, and artist name may be cached in our database to optimize Google Cloud Translation API calls. This cache is purely functional and is not associated with user accounts or used for tracking purposes. 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Services

UtaBridge uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: Used for user authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Translation API: Used to translate and romanize song metadata. Only the song title, album name, and artist name are sent to this service for processing.

8. Children's Privacy

UtaBridge is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
